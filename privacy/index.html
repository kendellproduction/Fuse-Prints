Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Who we are

Fuse Prints is a family-owned print shop based in Bradford, Pennsylvania. This page explains what we collect when you visit our site or contact us, and what we do with it.

What we collect

  • Information you give us: When you submit our quote request form, we collect your name, email, phone number (optional), project type, and message.
  • Automatic data: Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) used for security and basic analytics.

How we use it

  • To respond to quote requests and customer inquiries.
  • To produce, ship, and follow up on orders you place with us.
  • To improve the site and detect abuse.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Storage

Form submissions are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). Industry-standard encryption is used in transit and at rest. We retain inquiries indefinitely unless you request deletion.

Your rights

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing orders@fuseprints.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

The site uses essential cookies for functionality (e.g., admin sign-in). We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.
